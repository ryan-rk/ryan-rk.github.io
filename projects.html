DEEP LEARNING

Inverse Design Model

Research project utilizing the features-capturing capability of DCGAN and predictive properties of CNN to construct an inverse design model capable of designing the topology of microstructures. The related project files and journal papers can be found at Github site.

Mapping Network

A Resnet-based network developed to map the mechanical/material properties from coarse mesh to fine mesh, accelerating the training data generation process for the training of Neural Network-based surrogate model.

MapNet

An ANN-based surrogate model developed from the hybrid of SRResnet and U-net to perform mechanical field upscaling from coarse mesh (low resolution) to extremely fine mesh (high resolution). The model is used mainly to replace and accelerate the traditional structural design methods in mechanical field.
